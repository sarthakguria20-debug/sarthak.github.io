Our Story

Born from Bengaluru's Beautiful Chaos

Areca & Brew was born on the buzzing 12th Main, Indiranagar — right in the heart of Bengaluru's creative soul. Named after the areca palm that lines Karnataka's countryside, we're a love letter to Indian coffee culture, reimagined.

We source directly from small-batch roasters across Chikmagalur, Coorg, and the Nilgiris. Every cup tells the story of the soil it came from.
